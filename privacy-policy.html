Overview

CarPlay ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our CarPlay application (the "App") on Android devices with Android Automotive OS (AAOS).

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information.
  • Location Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any location or GPS data.
  • Usage Analytics: We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or behavioral analytics.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, hardware information, or system details.

Information We Process (But Do Not Store)

Audio Data: When you use Siri or make phone calls through CarPlay, the App temporarily processes audio data from your device's microphone. This audio is:
  • Never stored locally on your device
  • Never uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Immediately transmitted to the connected CarPlay dongle
  • Handled by Apple's CarPlay system and services

Video Stream: The App receives and displays video data from your iPhone through the CarPlay dongle. This video stream is never recorded or saved, never transmitted outside the local connection, and only displayed in real-time on your Android screen.

Touch Input: Touch gestures and interactions are converted to CarPlay commands and sent to your iPhone. No touch data is stored or analyzed.

How We Use Information

Audio Processing

  • Siri Requests: Audio is captured only when Siri is activated and immediately sent to Apple's Siri service via the CarPlay dongle
  • Phone Calls: Audio is captured only during active calls and transmitted to the caller through standard phone networks
  • No Voice Analysis: We do not analyze, process, or derive insights from voice data

CarPlay Functionality

  • Screen Mirroring: Video from your iPhone is displayed on your Android device screen
  • Touch Translation: Your touch inputs are converted to CarPlay commands for your iPhone
  • Media Control: Music and media controls are passed through to your iPhone's media apps

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Share Data: Since we don't collect or store personal information, there is no data to share with third parties.

Apple Services: Audio data processed during Siri interactions is handled by Apple's services according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or control over Apple's data handling.

No Third-Party Services: The App does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics services, or other third-party data collection services.

Data Security

  • No Data Storage: Since we don't store personal data, there are no databases or files containing your information that could be compromised.
  • Local Processing: All app functionality occurs locally on your device and through the direct USB connection to the CarPlay dongle.
  • Secure Communication: Communication between the App and CarPlay dongle uses standard CarPlay protocol encryption.

Permissions Explained

Microphone Permission (RECORD_AUDIO)

  • Purpose: Enable Siri voice commands and hands-free phone calls
  • Usage: Only active when Siri is triggered or during phone calls
  • Storage: Audio is never stored - only processed in real-time
  • Control: You can revoke this permission in Android Settings (this will disable Siri and phone calls)

Storage Permissions

  • Purpose: Read CarPlay dongle compatibility data and save app settings
  • Usage: Limited to app configuration files only
  • No Personal Data: We do not access photos, videos, documents, or other personal files

USB Host Permission

  • Purpose: Communicate with CarPlay dongles via USB
  • Usage: Required for core CarPlay functionality
  • Local Only: USB communication stays within your vehicle

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, this policy applies to all age groups.
